Cyber-Kinetic Defense

Modern smart grids are vulnerable to False Data Injection Attacks (FDIA) where attackers inject synthetic, realistic false sensor data to trick the grid's forecasting AI. Anahata protects against this. By executing within your secure, on-premises JVM, and requiring direct human-in-the-loop validation, Anahata prevents rogue agents from executing malicious or un-verified substation breaker actions, securing the physical system.
