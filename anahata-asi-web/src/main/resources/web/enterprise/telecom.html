The Shift to Sovereign Infrastructure

In 2026, leading telecom operators are transitioning into AICOs (AI Infrastructure Companies), deploying teams of autonomous agents to optimize Radio Access Networks (RAN). Managing these active agents requires deterministic orchestration and zero-trust boundaries. Anahata ASI is a local, JVM-native container designed to run securely within your network, ensuring total operational safety.

GCOT & 6G Compliance

The Global Coalition on Telecoms (GCOT) 6G Security and Resilience Principles (unveiled MWC 2026) mandate strict software containment and zero-trust permissions. By executing agents on local servers inside your firewalled network, Anahata completely prevents lateral privilege escalation, ensuring a single prompt-injection breach cannot cause systemic national network disruption.
