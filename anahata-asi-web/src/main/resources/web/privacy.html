The Anahata ASI Desktop and NetBeans Studio plugins are designed to operate completely offline and air-gapped. We collect absolutely zero telemetry, codebase analytics, prompt data, or usage metrics from your local execution.

We do not store or process your credit card information on our servers. Please refer to Paddle's Privacy Policy for detailed information on how they handle your payment data.
